# NNNN. Short title, imperative mood

Status: Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by [NNNN](NNNN-title.md)
Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What forces are at play — technical, business, or otherwise — that make this
a decision worth recording? What is the one-way door here: what does it cost
to reverse this later, and why is that cost higher than a normal PR revert?

## Decision

The change, stated plainly. One or two sentences.

## Consequences

What becomes easier or harder as a result. Include the real trade-offs, not
just the upside — an ADR that only argues for its own decision isn't useful
to the next reader trying to understand why.
